THE WORKFLOW

How HydroMate Works

A simple, end-to-end delivery flow from order to doorstep

01

Order Received

Customer places a water can order via the mobile app. Admin sees it instantly in the dashboard as a Pending order.

02

Staff Assigned

Admin approves and assigns the order to an available delivery staff member directly from the dashboard.

03

Water Delivered

Staff accepts, picks up the cans, and navigates to the customer's address. Status updates in real time across all views.

04

Order Completed

Staff enters the customer's 4-digit OTP to verify delivery. Order is marked Delivered and admin sees it instantly.
